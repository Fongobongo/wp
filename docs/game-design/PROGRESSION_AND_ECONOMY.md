# Progression, Economy, and F2P Rules

## 1. Unit Progression

### 1.1 Tiers
- Recruit
- Experienced
- Veteran
- Elite
- Legendary

### 1.2 Tree Structure
Each unit has two branches:
1. Faction branch:
- reinforces faction mechanics;
- adds thematic passives and triggers.
2. Role branch:
- reinforces role function (tank, burst, control, sustain, etc.);
- unlocks situational tactical options.

### 1.3 Final Node
- Top-node unlock grants a unique unit ability.
- Unique ability must change gameplay pattern, not only numeric values.

## 2. Duplicate and Max-Level Handling
1. Duplicates must always have value.
2. Allowed sink mechanics:
- recycle into resources;
- sell for soft currency;
- convert into progression materials.
3. For max-level units:
- prestige/evolution with long-term bonus;
- or dismantle into rare materials.

## 3. Monetization

### 3.1 Allowed
1. Cosmetics (skins, animations, banners, VFX).
2. Battle pass (acceleration/cosmetics/status).
3. NFT-based ownership and trading.
4. Secondary-market royalties.

### 3.2 Forbidden
1. Direct sale of combat power.
2. Exclusive stat bonuses gated behind real-money purchase.
3. Economy models where free players cannot compete in ranked.

## 4. Web3 Integration Rules
1. Fun-first before financial layer.
2. Chain logic must not block core gameplay loop speed.
3. Provable randomness applies outside real-time PvP simulation.
4. Critical PvP outcomes must not depend on on-chain latency.
5. Transaction costs must remain low and predictable.

## 5. Player Reward Systems
1. Daily and weekly quests.
2. Ranked and tournament rewards.
3. Seasonal ladder rewards.
4. Event-specific NFT/cosmetic drops.
5. Transparent reward distribution rules.

## 6. Economy Risks and Controls
1. Token inflation.
2. NFT speculative overheating.
3. Botting and multi-account abuse.
4. Value transfer from skill to wallet-size advantage.

Every season requires an economy audit:
- emission vs sink;
- retention by player segment;
- pay-to-win complaint share;
- conversion without fairness degradation.
